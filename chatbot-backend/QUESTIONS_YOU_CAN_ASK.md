## 💡 Pro Tips

### Tip 1: Ask Naturally
You don't need exact keywords. The chatbot understands natural questions!
- ✅ "What does Jahul do?"
- ✅ "Tell me about his work"
- ✅ "How can I contact him?"

### Tip 2: Be Specific
The more specific your question, the better the answer!
- Good: "What projects has he built?"
- Better: "Tell me about Visa2Fly"
- Best: "What's the approval rate of Visa2Fly?"

### Tip 3: Try Different Phrasings
If one question doesn't work, try rephrasing!
- "What's his experience?" → "How many years has he worked?"
- "What technologies?" → "What skills does he have?"
